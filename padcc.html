Opportunities with PADCC

PADCC offers students a chance to showcase their talent at Asia's Mega Dance Show (MDC), organized by Siri Muvva Arts. This platform not only allows performance but also introduces "Breaking Dance," set to debut as an Olympic sport at the 2024 Paris Olympics. In partnership with the Telangana Dance Sports Association, students can compete at District, State, and National levels based on their skills.
